D01B

Supporting documentation required

A copy of your passport indicating date of entry to Australia or an international movement record from the Department of Home Affairs. A record of attendance from each secondary school you attended during Year 11 and/or Year 12 or equivalent. The record of attendance must be issued on the school letterhead, be signed by the principal or another senior person at your school, and confirm the period you attended the school. It is not a record of absences.
